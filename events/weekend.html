Weekend Events

We host a few events on the weekend for families and any guests who stay during or over the weekend(s). We also host planned or booked events such as parties and small conventions or even family reunions or get togethers.

Most commonlyhosted events

  • Movie and Popcorn Night
  • Board and Card Game Night
  • Build your own sundae bar day
  • Make your own pizza day
  • Karaoke Night

Events can be requested when booking or held the day of. (Not all events are able to be prepared last minute please try to plan accordningly)
